Wheelchair Assessment and Training Services for Residential and Nursing Homes

Wheelchair assessments, advice and guidance for your residents

Do you have a resident who is not eligible for NHS provision? We offer private posture, seating and wheelchair assessments to ensure the equipment provided is suitable for the individuals long term needs.

Our Occupational Therapists offer guidance and support to residential and nursing homes by providing comprehensive assessments, interventions, and training in relation to:

  • Wheelchair and seating
  • Pressure management
  • Hoist/sling use
  • Equipment use
  • Manual Handling

Private Wheelchair Assessments

Individual Wheelchair Assessments: We can assess your residents' mobility, postural control and comfort needs to determine the most suitable wheelchair, cushion or equipment options for them.
Equipment Recommendation: We can recommend appropriate wheelchair and seating systems that meet the unique needs of each resident.

Pressure Management Assessments

Risk Assessment: We can conduct comprehensive pressure assessments to identify residents at risk of developing pressure ulcers.
Recommendation of Support Surfaces: We can recommend appropriate pressure-relieving cushions and other support surfaces to minimise the risk of pressure sores.

Hoist and Sling Assessments

Individualised Assessments: We can assess resident's mobility, weight-bearing abilities and specific needs to determine the appropriate type of hoist and sling.
Regular Reviews: We can conduct regular reviews to ensure that the chosen equipment continues to meet your resident's needs as their conditions may change over time.

Equipment Provision

Assessment and Prescription: We can assess the need for various assistive devices and equipment, such as grab bars, raised toilet seats and adaptive utensils.

Training

We provide opportunities for nursing and care staff to improve their knowledge and understanding on the principles of good seating, posture, and pressure management. We offer informative and practical training courses that involve getting hands-on with wheelchairs to enable staff to gain an understanding of regular issues with seating and positioning. Our courses give staff the awareness to help identify potential client issues and provide them with the skills to either address the concern themselves, if appropriate, or empower them to know who to contact for further advice and support. We also offer training in:

  • 24hr Posture Management
  • Pressure Care Management
  • Moving and Handling
  • Hoist/Sling Use

If your service has specific training requirements, please contact us regarding bespoke courses/ training.
