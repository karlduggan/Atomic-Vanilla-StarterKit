Private Wheelchair Assessments for Individuals

Independent wheelchair assessment in the comfort of your own home

Do you or a loved one need a wheelchair assessment and are not sure where to start?

Navigating wheelchair and equipment options can be like exploring a vast landscape of possibilities and finding the right solution can be both confusing and overwhelming. Amidst the diversity of wheelchairs available on the market, each with differing features and functionalities, it can be hard to know where to start.

We all have unique needs and preferences; therefore, it is important to ensure that any wheelchair or seating you choose is tailored to meet your own specific requirements. Factors such as your personal mobility needs, health considerations, posture and comfort, lifestyle, and the intended use of your wheelchair, all contribute to the complexity of the decision when purchasing a chair. All our Occupational Therapists are highly trained wheelchair assessors, with extensive experience in both and NHS and private assessments.

Why use an Occupational Therapist?

An Occupational Therapist (OT) is registered healthcare professionals offering assessments and support to people with physical, psychological, and social problems to enable them to live life to the fullest. Occupational Therapists are trained to assess your unique situation, providing expert advice on the most suitable solutions for you, including recommending equipment solutions or adaptations that can enhance your independence and wellbeing. Using an OT for a personalised wheelchair assessment can make the whole process of choosing the correct wheelchair, cushion, or seating system, easier and more tailored to your needs.

Why choose the Wheelchair OTs?

With over 24 years combined experience, specialising in the field of posture and seating, The Wheelchair OT's are experts in carrying out comprehensive mobility, seating and wheelchair assessments. Whether you are looking for a wheelchair for occasional or full-time use, our experts can provide professional, impartial assessment, and advice and recommendations to ensure your chosen wheelchair will meet your long-term needs. Our OT's can offer an independent wheelchair assessment at a time and place convenient to you, to carry out a thorough analysis of your needs, providing you with a detailed assessment report and recommendations on the most suitable wheelchair/equipment to meet your needs and requirements. This will enable you to approach a wheelchair supplier with confidence knowing that any equipment you purchase will be right for you.
