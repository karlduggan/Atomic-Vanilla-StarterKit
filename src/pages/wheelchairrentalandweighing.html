Private Wheelchair Rental and Weighing Services

Private Wheelchair Rental

In some scenarios, hiring a wheelchair can be a better option rather than buying equipment outright.

You may be unsure if the equipment is going to be right for you, therefore renting a chair can be a cost-effective way to ‘try before you buy’. You may also just want to hire it for a short period e.g., holidays, etc. Therefore, buying a wheelchair may not be the best solution. We are pleased to announce that The Wheelchair OTs are offering tilt in space wheelchairs for rental at a competitive price.

The wheelchairs are available for supply at short notice and will be set-up and handed over by one of our specialist therapists ensuring that the equipment is suitable to meet your needs. At the handover appointment our wheelchair OT ensure that you, your family, friends, and carers feel confident to manage the wheelchair and have all the information they need to use it. All our wheelchairs are to a high standard and specification. The wheelchair therapist will discuss with you what accessories may be needed to ensure good pressure and postural management enabling maximum comfort for you.

Our Wheelchair OT's are currently operating in the Southeast and not yet able to offer a ‘nationwide’ service. Please do contact us for further information and we will be happy to hear from you.

Client Weighing Service

Obtaining an accurate body weight can be important in managing your healthcare for many reasons, e.g., pre-surgical treatment, medication management, and equipment provision.

For some individuals, accessing weighing scales can be difficult, particularly if you are living with a long-term health condition that impacts your mobility. The Wheelchair OTs have access to both bed and wheelchair scales and can offer a home visit depending on your or your client’s needs.

For individuals or professionals requiring this service please do contact us.
